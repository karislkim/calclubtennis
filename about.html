California Club Tennis is a co-ed, student-run organization that practices and competes throughout the school year. We are a part of UC Berkeley's recreational sports organization, competing in USTA's Tennis on Campus league. We use World Team Tennis rules in our matches. In the fall we compete against Northern California university clubs like Stanford, UC Davis and Sac State. In the spring we travel to invitational tournaments, where we play the best club teams across the nation.

Hello! This is Cindy Liu and Anthony Busacca, your Cal Club Tennis captains for the 2017-2018 season. We have been on the team since our freshman year and can attest that it has been one of our greatest experiences at Berkeley. We can't wait to meet all of the great players trying out this fall and we're excited for the year we have ahead of us! GO BEARS!
